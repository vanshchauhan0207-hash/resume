Privacy Policy - Instant Chat

Last Updated: 03 December 2025

Welcome to Instant Chat. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data while providing a secure chat experience similar to WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, email, profile picture.
  • Contacts: Optional contact sync to identify friends using Instant Chat.
  • Messages: Text, voice messages, media (photos, videos, documents) sent through the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, IP address, and app usage data.
  • Location Data: Only if you enable location-based features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To send and receive messages, media, and notifications.
  • To sync contacts and show friends using Instant Chat.
  • To improve app functionality, performance, and user experience.
  • To ensure security and prevent abuse or fraud.

3. Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent, such as sharing profile information for friend connections.
  • With service providers who help operate the app (for example: cloud storage providers, notification services).
  • To comply with legal requirements, enforce our Terms of Service, or protect our users’ safety.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including end-to-end encryption for messages. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Message Retention

Messages and media sent through Instant Chat are stored securely on our servers for delivery purposes. Delivered messages may remain encrypted on your device and our servers until deleted by you. We recommend regularly backing up important chats if desired.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request access to your personal data stored by Instant Chat.
  • You can request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.
  • You can manage permissions such as contact sync, location, and notification preferences in your device settings.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and performance monitoring. This helps us improve app functionality and detect errors or security issues.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at: support@instantchat.com
